About Me

Hello! I'm Arka, a passionate web developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy creating interactive and user-friendly websites, blending creativity with functionality. My focus is on front-end development, but I am also exploring backend technologies to build full-stack applications.

With a keen interest in problem-solving and competitive programming, I constantly challenge myself to improve my skills. Whether it's designing elegant UI components or optimizing website performance, I am always looking for ways to innovate and enhance the user experience.

Beyond coding, I have a deep passion for esports and gaming, particularly in the Free Fire community. I have worked on projects related to tournament hosting, scrim management, and esports team websites, integrating technologies like Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB.
